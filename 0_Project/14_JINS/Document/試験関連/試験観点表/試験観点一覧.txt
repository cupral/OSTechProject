必須パラメータ

・callbackパラメータ不一致
jins-qr://scan?call=https://hoge1234.com/tw?uuid=xxxx&mode=2&titlestr=TitleTest&okstr=OKTest&cancelstr=CancelTest&cameramode=2
jins-qr://scan?mode=2&titlestr=TitleTest&okstr=OKTest&cancelstr=CancelTest&cameramode=2

任意パラメータ
・その他パラメータ不一致
jins-qr://scan?callback=https://hoge1234.com/tw?uuid=xxxx
jins-qr://scan?callback=https://hoge1234.com/tw?uuid=xxxx&mmdd=2&title=TitleTest&ok=OKTest&cancel=CancelTest&camera=2

・modeパラメータ不一致
jins-qr://scan?callback=https://hoge1234.com/tw?uuid=xxxx&mmdd=1&titlestr=TitleTest&okstr=OKTest&cancelstr=CancelTest&cameramode=2

・titlestrパラメータ不一致
jins-qr://scan?callback=https://hoge1234.com/tw?uuid=xxxx&mode=2&title=TitleTest&okstr=OKTest&cancelstr=CancelTest&cameramode=2

・okstrパラメータ不一致
jins-qr://scan?callback=https://hoge1234.com/tw?uuid=xxxx&mode=2&titlestr=TitleTest&ok=OKTest&cancelstr=CancelTest&cameramode=2

・cancelstrパラメータ不一致
jins-qr://scan?callback=https://hoge1234.com/tw?uuid=xxxx&mode=2&titlestr=TitleTest&okstr=OKTest&cancel=CancelTest&cameramode=2

・cameramodeパラメータ不一致
jins-qr://scan?callback=https://hoge1234.com/tw?uuid=xxxx&mode=2&titlestr=TitleTest&okstr=OKTest&cancelstr=CancelTest&camera=1

必須パラメータ値
・callback不正
jins-qr://scan?callback=abcdefg&mode=2&titlestr=TitleTest&okstr=OKTest&cancelstr=CancelTest&cameramode=2

・callback不正
jins-qr://scan?callback=&mode=2&titlestr=TitleTest&okstr=OKTest&cancelstr=CancelTest&cameramode=2

任意パラメータ値
・mode不正
jins-qr://scan?callback=https://hoge1234.com/tw?uuid=xxxx&mode=3&titlestr=TitleTest&okstr=OKTest&cancelstr=CancelTest&cameramode=2

・mode空
jins-qr://scan?callback=https://hoge1234.com/tw?uuid=xxxx&mode=&titlestr=TitleTest&okstr=OKTest&cancelstr=CancelTest&cameramode=2

・titlestr不正
jins-qr://scan?callback=https://hoge1234.com/tw?uuid=xxxx&mode=2&titlestr=1&okstr=OKTest&cancelstr=CancelTest&cameramode=2

・titlestr空
jins-qr://scan?callback=https://hoge1234.com/tw?uuid=xxxx&mode=2&titlestr=&okstr=OKTest&cancelstr=CancelTest&cameramode=2

・okstr不正
jins-qr://scan?callback=https://hoge1234.com/tw?uuid=xxxx&mode=2&titlestr=TitleTest&okstr=1&cancelstr=CancelTest&cameramode=2

・okstr空
jins-qr://scan?callback=https://hoge1234.com/tw?uuid=xxxx&mode=2&titlestr=TitleTest&okstr=&cancelstr=CancelTest&cameramode=2

・cancelstr不正
jins-qr://scan?callback=https://hoge1234.com/tw?uuid=xxxx&mode=2&titlestr=TitleTest&okstr=OKTest&cancelstr=1&cameramode=2

・cancelstr空
jins-qr://scan?callback=https://hoge1234.com/tw?uuid=xxxx&mode=2&titlestr=TitleTest&okstr=OKTest&cancelstr=&cameramode=2

・cameramode不正
jins-qr://scan?callback=https://hoge1234.com/tw?uuid=xxxx&mode=2&titlestr=TitleTest&okstr=OKTest&cancelstr=CancelTest&cameramode=3

・cameramode空
